Welcome to the online world, where communication happens in the blink of an eye and friendships are forged through screens. Maintaining proper hydration is crucial for overall health and well-being. Our bodies rely on water to perform essential functions, such as regulating temperature, lubricating joints, and delivering nutrients to cells. Dehydration can lead to fatigue, headaches, and decreased cognitive function.
